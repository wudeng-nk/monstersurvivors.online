How to Play Monster Survivors

Getting Started: Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character around the battlefield. Your character automatically attacks nearby enemies, so focus on positioning and survival.

Survival Strategy: Avoid getting overwhelmed by monster hordes. Keep moving to prevent enemies from surrounding you, and prioritize collecting experience gems dropped by defeated monsters to level up.

Power-ups: When you level up, choose from various upgrades including new weapons, increased damage, improved speed, and defensive abilities. Each choice affects your survival strategy.

Advanced Tips: Learn enemy movement patterns, manage your positioning carefully, and experiment with different upgrade combinations to find your preferred playstyle. The key to high scores is surviving as long as possible while eliminating maximum monsters.
